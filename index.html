Team Profile

While we've only been together as a team for one year, we have accomplished a lot. We've gone through three major revisions of our bot, countless autonomous functions, and attended several tournaments. In June 2013, we were the winning alliance at the Rex Toss Up Tournament in Maryland. In December 2013, we were tournament finalists at the Monroe Township High School Tournament and in February of 2014 we were on the winning alliance of the New Jersey State Championship, stamping our ticket to the 2014 World Championships in Anaheim, California.
